## Vision
Let's Master Spanish — an educational blog empowering Spanish language learners from beginner to fluent through proven strategies, high-quality lessons, and community engagement. Target audience: Adult learners seeking structured, accessible Spanish education online.

## Design
Modern EdTech aesthetic — professional confidence with warm Mediterranean optimism. Clean, whitespace-heavy layout prioritizing readability and conversion. Sister site to learnspanishlikeidid.com.

Color system (HSL mapped to shadcn tokens):
- `--primary: 15 75% 60%` (Warm Terracotta #e6734d — CTAs, category tags, brand accent)
- `--background: 39 59% 98%` (Soft Cream #fdfbf7 — main background)
- `--muted: 39 40% 96%` (Light Cream tint — section backgrounds)
- `--foreground: 224 64% 33%` (Mediterranean Blue #1e3a8a — body text, deep secondary)
- `--secondary: 224 64% 33%` (Mediterranean Blue — secondary accent)
- `--accent: 15 75% 60%` (Warm Terracotta — badges, alerts, announcement bar)
- `--card: 0 0% 100%` (white cards)
- `--border: 39 30% 90%` (subtle cream borders)

Typography:
- Headings: Montserrat (Bold, 700) — geometric authority
- Body: Open Sans (Regular, 400) — 16px base, 1.6 line-height

Style: Flat design, 8px border-radius on buttons/cards/images, high contrast for accessibility. Warm, inviting Mediterranean palette.

## Features
Homepage with sticky header (social icons, search, logo, nav: Home/Spanish Basics/Grammar/Vocabulary/Reviews/About), hero section (lifestyle image, headline, large CTA), 2-column main body (70% Recent Articles feed, 30% sidebar with About/Popular Lessons/Newsletter opt-in), 3-card Featured Grid (Beginner Path/Verb Conjugation/Travel Phrases), Testimonials slider, floating announcement bar (Terracotta), 3-column footer (mission/quick links/social). Mobile-responsive, lazy-loading images, SEO-optimized headings.
